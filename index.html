What's Your Issues?


















What is the purpose of this website and the e-mobility bus service?

Our website serves as a platform to provide real-time updates, facilitate issue reporting, and offer information about our enhanced e-mobility bus service. Our goal is to make public transportation more efficient, sustainable, and user-friendly.

How does the e-mobility bus service differ from traditional bus services?

Our e-mobility bus service focuses on sustainability by utilizing electric buses, reducing carbon emissions, and providing a cleaner and quieter commuting experience. We also integrate advanced technologies for real-time updates and issue reporting.

How can I find information about e-mobility bus routes on the website?

Navigate to the "Routes" section on our website to access detailed information about our e-mobility bus routes, including stops, schedules, and real-time updates.

Can I report issues specific to the e-mobility buses?

Yes, you can report issues related to e-mobility buses through the "Report an Issue" feature on our website. Your feedback is crucial in ensuring the optimal functioning of our e-mobility bus fleet.

How can I stay informed about the real-time status of e-mobility buses?

To receive real-time updates on the status of e-mobility buses, visit the homepage or log in to your account. You can also enable personalized notifications for your preferred e-mobility bus routes through your account settings.
